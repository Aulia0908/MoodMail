Application Programmer Firyal Chalissa Asyla Application programmers are people who create applications for computers or mobile devices. In addition, they also perform testing and repair (debugging) if there are errors in the program.
Content Writer Satria Bintang Jarel A. Content writers are people who write content in the form of articles, captions, product descriptions, or website content. In addition, they can also apply SEO techniques so that writing is easier to find in search engines.
Graphic Designer Alliya Puti Humaira Graphic designers are people who create visual designs for various purposes, such as posters, logos, application displays, or social media.
